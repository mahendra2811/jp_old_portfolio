About

Hello myself Jagdish Pooniya , I am a banker by profession. However, my true passions lie in wildlife photography and cricket .

Growing up in a small village in the Jodhpur district of Rajasthan, I developed a deep appreciation for the natural world around me. As I got older, I realized that I had a talent for capturing the beauty of this world through the lens of my camera.

I started pursuing photography as a serious hobby while still working in PNB bank , and over time I became known as a skilled and dedicated wildlife photographer. Living in Rajasthan has given me ample opportunities to photograph some of India's most stunning creatures, from Great indian bustered , Tigers , Leopards to birds and reptiles.

Of course, cricket remains another of my great passions. In my free time, you're likely to find me on the field, either playing with my local team or coaching younger players. To me, both cricket and photography are about dedication, hard work, and a commitment to excellence.
